Installing your signature in Outlook

Choose your platform below

1
Fill in your details and click Generate My Signature.
2

3
In Outlook, go to Outlook menu → Preferences → Signatures → +. Name it "Simpliphy".
4
Click Edit, select all (Cmd+A), delete, then paste (Cmd+V).
5
Set as default for New Messages and Replies, then send yourself a test email.
